Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our tours and services

What is included in the tour price?

Our tour prices typically include transportation, guide services, entrance fees to attractions, and meals as specified in the tour description. Accommodation is included for multi-day tours.

Do you offer custom tours?

Yes, we specialize in creating custom itineraries tailored to your interests, schedule, and budget. Contact us with your requirements and we'll design the perfect Ghanaian adventure for you.

What is the best time to visit Ghana?

Ghana has a tropical climate with two main seasons: wet and dry. The dry season (November to March) is generally the best time to visit, especially for wildlife viewing and outdoor activities.

Is it safe to travel in Ghana?

Ghana is considered one of the safest countries in Africa. We prioritize your safety and provide experienced guides who ensure your journey is both enjoyable and secure.
